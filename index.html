What Are STEM Majors?

STEM is an acronym that stands for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. These fields are interconnected and focus on analytical approaches to problem-solving and innovation.

S

Science

T

Technology

E

Engineering

M

Mathematics

STEM majors develop essential skills like critical thinking, problem-solving, data analysis, and technical expertise that are highly valued in today's job market.
