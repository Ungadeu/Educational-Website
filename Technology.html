Fun Tech Facts

Imagine living a long, long time ago, like when Benjamin Franklin was around! He didn't just discover electricity; he invented things like the lightning rod to keep houses safe, the Franklin stove to keep people warm, and even a musical instrument called the glass armonica! These were super cool inventions for his time, just like your tablet is cool now. Over time, people learned from these ideas and made them even better, like how we have electric heaters instead of Franklin stoves today.

Now, think about how much we use technology every day! We chat with friends on social media, order our favorite toys online, and play awesome video games. Sometimes, people even use super-smart computers called AI to solve problems, even when it might be easier to do it ourselves. But remember, we can also use technology to do amazing things, like build robots that clean up pollution or create apps that help people learn new things. We can make the world a better place with technology!
